Piwik is an open source (GPL license) web analytics software. It gives interesting reports on your website visitors, your popular pages, the search engines keywords they used, the language they speak and so much more.

Please visit piwik.org for download and more information.

Piwik must be installed and working at some place in order to use this plugin.

Your Piwik URL
Type here the URL of your Piwik installation.
Your Piwik Token
You'll find your Piwik token by clicking on "API" in Piwik interface. It consists of 32 letters and digits.

Once you correctly filled those two fields, you will be able to choose a site you previously created in Piwik. If you need to create a new site in Piwik for your blog you can use the Create a new Piwik site for this blog form.

You can prevent tracking of your own visits by adding IP address(es) in the field Do not track following IP addresses. This is a space separated list.

If you don't like the way Piwik display your page names, you can check Use fancy page names.
